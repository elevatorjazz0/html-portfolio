Elevator Jazz's Movie Recommendations!

Some movie recommendations from your one and only.

Whiplash.

Love everyhting about this movie. The acting, cinematography, story, the jazz etc. Sick movie!
My absolute favorite movie of all time.

Nayattu.

A Malayalam movie that is gross, intense and thrilling. Filled with an array of great actors,
great cinematography and great screenplay, this movie is a treat to watch!

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara.

One of the few times I've been amazed by a Bollywood movie is Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara!
An amazing movie about friendship, love and everything in between. And, the soundtrack
is absolutely gorgeous and, in my opinion, clears any commercial music that is released for
movies now-a-days.
